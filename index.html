About Amateur Photo Prompt Generator

This tool generates prompts for Stable Diffusion's RealisticVisionV60B1_v51HyperVAE model that create intentionally amateur-looking photos rather than professional ones.

The prompts include various amateur photography characteristics like poor lighting, common mistakes, old equipment, and imperfect composition to create authentic everyday photos rather than professional-quality images.

Perfect for generating images that look like they were taken by regular people in daily life situations.

Tips:

  • Adjust the Amateur Level slider to control how amateur the photos will look
  • Use the history tab to see your previously generated prompts
  • Save your favorite prompts to reuse them later
  • Custom subjects work great with the random button
